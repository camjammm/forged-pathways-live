Student Intake Process

We're excited that you're considering joining Forged Pathways. Our intake process is designed to ensure each student receives the support they need to succeed.

Who Can Apply?

We welcome formerly incarcerated individuals who are ready to commit to positive change through skilled trades training. No prior welding experience is required.

How to Apply

  1. Download and complete the intake form.
  2. Submit the completed form by email or drop it off at our center.
  3. Attend an intake interview and orientation session.
