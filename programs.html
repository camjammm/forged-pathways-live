Welding Certification Program

Our flagship 6-month training program covers MIG, TIG, and Stick welding. Students receive hands-on experience, safety training, and real-world practice in a professional-grade shop environment.

Career Readiness Support

Resume workshops, mock interviews, job placement assistance, and employer networking events help students transition into the workforce with confidence.

Rec Room + Mental Health Support

We provide a student rec room for social connection and decompression, plus access to licensed counselors and peer mentors who understand the challenges of reentry.

Graduation & Certification

Graduates earn industry-recognized welding certifications and leave the program with a resume, reference letters, and the option to keep their PPE and tools.
